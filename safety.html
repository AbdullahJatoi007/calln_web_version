Safety Tips

Calln is built for anonymous conversations. Your safety depends on how you use it. Follow these simple rules to stay protected while chatting.

🛡️ General Safety

  • Never share your real name, phone number, or address
  • Keep conversations anonymous at all times
  • Trust your instinct — leave any call that feels unsafe
  • Use the “End Call” button immediately if needed

🚫 What to Avoid

  • Do not share personal or financial information
  • Avoid clicking unknown links from strangers
  • Do not agree to meet people in real life
  • Do not send or request inappropriate content

⚠️ Reporting Abuse

  • Use the Report button during or after a call
  • Block users who behave inappropriately
  • Email abuse reports to support@getcalln.com
  • Serious violations may lead to permanent bans

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use country filters if you want more control
  • Stay polite — better conversations last longer
  • Disconnect anytime without explanation
  • Use Calln in a private, safe environment
