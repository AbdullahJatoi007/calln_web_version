What is Calln?

Calln is a free, anonymous voice calling platform that connects you instantly with real people from around the world. There are no accounts to create, no personal information to share, and no downloads required. You simply open the app or website, press Start, and within seconds you are speaking with someone new.

Unlike traditional social platforms where you are judged by your profile picture, follower count, or personal history, Calln strips all of that away. The only thing that matters is your voice and what you have to say. This creates a uniquely honest and refreshing kind of conversation — the kind that is increasingly rare in today's curated social media world.

Calln is available on the web at getcalln.com and as a free Android app on the Google Play Store. Both platforms share the same technology and the same user pool, so you are always connected to the largest possible community of people regardless of which platform you use.

Calln is built around a small set of carefully designed features that make every conversation better. Nothing is added for the sake of complexity — every feature solves a real problem that anonymous callers face.

Why People Choose Calln

There are dozens of platforms for meeting strangers online, but most of them have fundamental problems that Calln was specifically designed to solve.

Video chat platforms create enormous visual pressure. People feel judged by their appearance, their surroundings, their lighting. Many users — particularly those who are shy, introverted, or simply private — find video calls deeply uncomfortable. Calln removes the camera entirely. You are just a voice, and that single change makes conversations dramatically more honest and relaxed.

Text-only platforms lack the warmth and nuance of real human communication. A voice conveys emotion, humour, sarcasm, and personality in ways that text simply cannot. Calln keeps it voice-first while still offering text chat as a complement, not a replacement.

Since the closure of Omegle in late 2023, millions of users have been searching for a safe, reliable alternative. Calln fills that gap with modern technology, better safety features, and a commitment to privacy that older platforms never offered. There are no logs, no recordings, and no data sold to advertisers.

Your Privacy on Calln

Privacy is not an afterthought at Calln — it is the foundation the entire platform is built on. We believe that people have the right to have genuine conversations without being tracked, profiled, or recorded.

Calln does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any personal information. Your voice is transmitted directly to your partner using WebRTC peer-to-peer technology, which means our servers never receive or process your audio. The moment your call ends, all associated data — including any chat messages or shared images — is permanently deleted from memory. There is nothing to retrieve, nothing to leak, and nothing to sell.

The only data we temporarily hold is your IP address, used to match you with users in your preferred region and to enforce our community safety ban system against reported users. IP addresses are not stored beyond your active session. For full details, read our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Calln completely free?

Yes. Calln is free to use on both the website and the Android app. There are no subscriptions, no premium tiers, and no hidden fees. The website is supported by advertising. The mobile app is currently ad-free.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Calln does not have accounts. You do not need to register, log in, or share any personal information to start a call. Just open the site or app and press Start.

Are my calls recorded?

No. Voice audio travels directly between you and your partner via WebRTC peer-to-peer technology. Our servers never receive, record, or store any audio. It is technically impossible for us to record your calls.

Can I choose who I talk to?

You can filter by country to increase the chance of matching with someone from a specific region. Within that filter, matching is random and anonymous. You cannot choose a specific person to call.

What happens when I report someone?

Tapping the Report button immediately disconnects the call and issues a 24-hour IP ban to the reported user. Repeated or severe violations result in a permanent ban. In cases involving illegal content, we cooperate with law enforcement.

Is Calln available on iPhone?

Calln works on Safari on iPhone through the website at getcalln.com. The dedicated mobile app is currently Android-only. An iOS app may be available in the future.
