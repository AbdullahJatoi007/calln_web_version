FAQ — Calln

Is Calln free to use?

Yes. Calln is completely free with no hidden charges or subscriptions.

Do I need an account?

No account is required. You can start instantly.

Are calls recorded?

No. Calls are not recorded and remain private between users.

Can I choose countries?

Yes. You can filter users by country or go worldwide.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Calln works on all modern mobile and desktop browsers.

Is Calln safe?

We use reporting tools, moderation, and age restriction (18+) to keep the platform safe.
